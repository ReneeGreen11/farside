**Joining the Mailing List**

You’ll receive no more than one email per month. Each email will simply highlight any new posts and include links so you can read them easily.

Please make sure to open the email when it arrives, and check your spam or promotions folders if you don’t see it. If you don’t plan to read these emails, it’s better not to join the list. You can always visit the website directly to see the latest posts.

If you do join, I’m glad to have you here, and I hope you find the content useful and worth your time.
